About Me

I am Hongzheng Li, a senior student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin - Madison. I have previously studied at the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities and the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. With a strong foundation in systems and software engineering, I have honed my skills through internships and hands-on projects, including my past role at Emerson Electric Co., where I contribute to error correction in engineering graphs and daily staffing processes.

My technical expertise spans C, C++, Python, and web technologies, along with a deep understanding of memory management and operating systems. I am passionate about solving complex software challenges and am always seeking opportunities to learn and innovate.
